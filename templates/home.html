A. N. Mitra Memorial Meeting and Lecture

In the loving memory of and as a tribute to the life and work of Prof. Asoke Nath Mitra, we are organizing this meeting and the second memorial lecture. Prof. Mitra's contributions to the field of High Energy and Nuclear Physics are recognised widely around the world and have inspired many generations of physicists. The inaugural memorial lecture was given by Prof. N. Panchapakesan on Apr 17, 2023, and was organized by the department of Physics, University of Delhi.
