A. N. Mitra Memorial Meeting and Lecture

In the loving memory of and as a tribute to the life and work of Prof. Asoke Nath Mitra, we are organizing this meeting and the second memorial lecture. Prof. Mitra's contributions to the field of High Energy and Nuclear Physics are recognised widely around the world and have inspired many generations of physicists.

April 14th-15th, 2025

Meeting Lecture
 

2nd A. N. Mitra Memorial Lecture

This memorial lecture will be delivered by Professor Edward Witten on the topic "A Background - Independent Algebra For Gravity". This is the second A. N. Mitra memorial lecture. The inaugural memorial lecture was given by Prof. N. Panchapakesan on Apr 17, 2023, and was organized by the department of Physics, University of Delhi.
